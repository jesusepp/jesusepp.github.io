Courses
University Courses
12 weeks duration each
  • MC2131 - Dibujo Mecánico (Technical Drawing)
    Description: Introductory course to mechanical drawings. Topics covered are: orthogonal and isometric projections, technical drawing rules and an introduction to computer-aided design.
    More information at: MC2131

  • MC2141 / MC2142 / MC2143 - Mecánica de Materiales I, II y III (Mechanics of Materials I, II and III)
    Description: this three courses build the concepts of how elastic materials behave to some load conditions. Topics coverd are: strain and stresses, beam flexion, theories of failure, combine loads and fatigue failure, among others.
    More information at: MC2141 / MC2142 / MC2143

  • MC4131 - Diseño de Máquinas I (Machine Design I)
    Description: focusing on mechanical design thinking, this course introduce a first oportunity for the students to work with some of the common mechanical systems that are used in mechanical design. Topics covered are: tolerances and manufacturing processes, shaft-hub coupling, welded joins and bolted joins.
    More information at: MC4131

  • MC4133 - Metodología del Diseño (Design Methodology)
    Description: course designed to build some of the important design philosophies that are use in the current world. Topics covered are: the design process, design methodology, design tools and intelectual propertie, among others.
    More information at: MC4133

Online
  • A Hands-on Introduction to Engineering Simulations - (6 weeks, 4-6 hours/week)
    Description: Introduction to the Engineering Simulation world through a hands-on course that lets the student practice this type of analysis with the software ANSYS.
    Available at: edx.org
